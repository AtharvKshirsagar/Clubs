Literati is one of the most active, entertaining and intellectually stimulating clubs in the institute, with a healthy mixture of formal (meaning you probably stand to get prize money) and informal (meaning you get chocolates instead!) events held quite frequently. We host a range of events like Quizzes, Creative Writing, Rebuses, Word Games, Scrabble, Scavenger Hunts, Treasure Hunts and Pot-pourri (dumb charades and other fun games)
